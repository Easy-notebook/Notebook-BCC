# model_evaluation Step Catalog

## step_1_cross_validation_assessment

Goal: 通过合适的交叉验证方案评估泛化性能

Verified Artifacts:
- cv_plan
- cv_results
- metric_summary
Acceptance:
- - 输出均值/方差与置信估计；记录随机种子

## step_2_holdout_test_evaluation

Goal: 在未见数据上评估并量化泛化差距

Verified Artifacts:
- holdout_results
- error_analysis
Acceptance:
- - 输出主要指标（RMSE/R²/MAE）与误差分布

## step_3_residual_and_error_analysis

Goal: 深入诊断残差/误差结构与偏差，提出改进方向

Verified Artifacts:
- residual_analysis
- improvement_hypotheses
Acceptance:
- - 输出异方差/自相关/偏度等诊断与建议

## step_4_pcs_validation

Goal: 进行可预测性/可计算性/稳定性的综合验证（含扰动实验）

Verified Artifacts:
- perturbation_results
- pcs_validation_report
Acceptance:
- - 输出扰动设计、结果与稳定性结论
