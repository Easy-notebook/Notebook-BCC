# model_selection Step Catalog

## step_1_baseline_models

Goal: 训练一组简洁、稳定、可解释的基线模型作为对照

Verified Artifacts:
- baseline_models
- baseline_metrics
Acceptance:
- - 至少 2–3 个不同类型的基线训练与评估完成

## step_2_algorithm_suitability_assessment

Goal: 根据数据特征/业务约束评估主流算法适配度

Verified Artifacts:
- candidate_models
- suitability_report
Acceptance:
- - 给出 ≥2 类不同算法的适配分析与取舍理由

## step_3_model_comparison_and_selection

Goal: 统一评估候选模型并给出选择结论与理由

Verified Artifacts:
- model_comparison_report
- selected_model_name
- model_selection_rationale
Acceptance:
- - 测试 ≥3 个模型；输出 selected_model_name 与充分理由
