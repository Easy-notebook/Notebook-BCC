================================================================================
STEP 4.4: METHODOLOGY STRATEGY CONSOLIDATION (方法策略整合)
================================================================================

STAGE: Methodology Strategy Formulation
GOAL: 整合所有策略组件，形成完整的建模方法论

PCS CONSIDERATIONS:
- Predictability: 确保整体策略优先考虑泛化能力
- Computability: 验证整体流程的实施可行性
- Stability: 确保策略在不同场景下的稳定性

================================================================================
BEHAVIORS IN THIS STEP:
================================================================================
1. behavior_1_strategy_integration.txt
   - 整合特征工程、模型选择、训练评估策略

2. behavior_2_pcs_framework_integration.txt
   - 将PCS框架全面集成到策略中

3. behavior_3_risk_assessment_and_mitigation.txt
   - 识别和缓解技术和业务风险

4. behavior_4_implementation_plan_development.txt
   - 制定详细的实施计划

================================================================================
OVERALL OUTPUT ARTIFACTS:
================================================================================
- integrated_methodology_document: 整合方法论文档
- end_to_end_process_diagram: 端到端流程图
- component_coordination_plan: 组件协调计划
- process_optimization_report: 流程优化报告
- pcs_integration_framework: PCS集成框架
- predictability_assurance_plan: 可预测性保证计划
- computability_verification: 可计算性验证报告
- stability_testing_framework: 稳定性测试框架
- technical_risk_register: 技术风险登记册
- risk_mitigation_strategies: 风险缓解策略
- business_risk_assessment: 业务风险评估
- contingency_plans: 应急计划
- phased_implementation_plan: 分阶段实施计划
- milestone_schedule: 里程碑时间表

- final_methodology_strategy: 最终方法策略文档
